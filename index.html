Traditional martial arts also contribute to the development of techniques and tactics in MMA. In addition, traditional fighting styles such as Karate and Taekwondo have also changed and adapted to reflect the needs of MMA. For example, the high kick from Taekwondo has become a powerful weapon when integrated into the tactics of many MMA fighters.
